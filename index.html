➊ Support

A new age fitness brand, created in collaboration with Michael Q and Aja Bivens, designed to address the sweet seduction of cult branding in a world of useless products.

➋ I'll Show U Mine

A book featuring essays by Max Bill, Jan Tschichold, and Max Renner, interrupted by Paris Hilton. Lyrics, quotes, and blurred photos of Hilton are included to show her image is as influential and relevant to my design practice as typographic essays.

➌ Sarah Sze

Booklet and poster designed for an exhibition by Sarah Sze. Small modular pieces were used to complement the installation without distracting from its intricacies.

➍ Rebote

A promotional video for a table tennis night club.

➎ BYOB

Clothing made in collaboration with Michael Q that focuses on functionality. Comfort, storage, and durability were all considered while designing the school uniform inspired pieces.
